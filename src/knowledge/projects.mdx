### Mintmark — Personal Branding & Knowledge Platform

**What it does:**
A passive-first personal branding platform that turns what you learn into platform-ready content for LinkedIn, X, and Medium — simultaneously. Features a unified activity heatmap aggregating GitHub, LeetCode, notes, and sessions, AI-powered content generation via BYOK, and a RAG-powered personal knowledge assistant scoped strictly to your own notes and posts.

**Problem it solved:**
Developers learn constantly but rarely document or publish what they learn — the activation energy for "write a LinkedIn post" is just too high. Mintmark removes that friction by meeting you where learning already happens and generating platform-ready drafts automatically.

### Syncify — YouTube → Spotify Playlist Converter

**What it does:**
Converts YouTube playlists to Spotify using intelligent fuzzy matching. Handles OAuth 2.0 flows for both platforms and gracefully manages API rate limits.

**Problem it solved:**
Migrating music playlists between platforms was entirely manual — search every song, find the closest match, add it. Syncify automates the whole flow including imperfect title matches.

**My role:**
Solo developer. Built end-to-end from OAuth plumbing to the fuzzy matching algorithm.

**Tech stack:**
React.js, Node.js, OAuth 2.0, YouTube Data API, Spotify Web API, fuzzy matching
